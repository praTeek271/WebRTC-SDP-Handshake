WebRTC SDP Handshaking

WebRTC SDP Demo

Follow the steps below to pass SDP offer and answer between two users:

  1. Step 1: User 1, click "Create Offer" to generate an SDP offer and copy it from the text area below.
  2. Step 2: User 2, paste the SDP offer generated by User 1 into the text area and click "Create Answer" to generate an SDP answer and copy it from the text area below.
  3. Step 3: User 1, paste the SDP answer generated by User 2 into the text area and click "Add Answer."
